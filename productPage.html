About the designer

Luis Novo

Luis Novo is a Portuguese aspiring furniture designer, born in Esposende. With a profound passion for craftsmanship and a love for fine wine, he brings a unique perspective to his work. Luis's journey into furniture design began in his humble garage, where he honed his skills, transforming wood into functional works of art. Drawing inspiration from his surroundings and cultural heritage, Luis seeks to create furniture pieces that tell a story and enhance the spaces they inhabit. His designs blend tradition with innovation, resulting in timeless yet contemporary pieces that captivate the senses. In addition to his dedication to furniture design, Luis has a deep appreciation for wine. His exploration of flavors and traditions adds depth to his creative process, infusing his designs with an extra layer of richness and complexity. As the founder of the Nofir brand, Luis's vision and craftsmanship shine through in every piece, representing the culmination of his passion for design and his commitment to quality.
